Aquatic Classes

Home
  • One-On-One Swimming Lessons

    One-on-one swimming lessons are currently available by appointment. Please call to schedule swimming lessons today!

  • Aqua Fun and Fit

    This is a great class for those looking to incorporate low-impact cardiovascular and toning training into their fitness plan. Morning classes are held in the shallow end of our lap pool and evening classes are held in our heated therapy pool.

  • Aqua Power

    The only NO IMPACT fitness class available. Held in the deep end of our pool this class will have you doing water aerobics while treading water.

  • Arthritis Foundation Aquatic Program

    This class is designed to help you improve the funcation and performance of your daily tasks, mobility, independence, flexibility, balance, and coordination. This class is held in our heated therapy pool, is taught by a certified AFAP instructor, and is great for beginners and those suffering from Arthritis, Fibromyalgia, and MS.

  • Senior Splash

    A water fitness class that can be as hard, or as easy, as you want to make it. Held in our heated therapy pool this class focuses on cardio, flexibility, and balance. A great class for arthritis and fibromyalgia sufferers.

Silver Sneakers

Home

Somerset Family Fitness Center is excited to offer the SilverSneakers Fitness Program, the nation's leading exercise program designed exclusively for older adults. SilverSneakers includes an unlimited, free, membership that includes the use of the fitness equipment, indoor pools, fitness classes, and other amenities, optional low-impact SilverSneakers classes taught by certified instructors, monthly social events and much more!

Have fun and move to the music through a variety of exercises designed to increase muscular strength, range of movement, and activity for daily living skills. Hand held weights, elastic tubing with handles, and a ball are offered for resistance; and a chair is used for seated and/or standing support.

This program is designed to provide a fun and challenging way for you to continue the lifelong journey of holistic health. The program is easily personalized to address multiple fitness levels and differences in health needs. Contact us to find out if your Supplemental Medicare health plan includes SilverSneakers at no additional cost to you.

Facility Policies

Home

Welcome to the SomersetFamilyFitnessCenter, we are so glad you are here! Everyone who enters our facility is considered a valuable member of our SFFC family. This includes the people in our many programs, the volunteers who donate their time and talents, those who support us throughout the community and, of course all of our valued members. Whatever your goals may be by joining our facility, our staff wants to help you achieve them!

Member Services and Amenities

Members of our facility have full access to our weight room, gymnasium, outdoor walking track, basketball court, Kid Zone, swimming pools, hot tub, sauna, steam room, and non-fee based fitness classes. Additionally, you will receive discounted rates on most of our fee based classes and an annual allotment of guest passes to share with family and friends.

Locker Rental

Members may rent a small locker for $5 monthly or a large locker for $10 per month. Locks are provided by the facility or if you choose to do so, you may provide your own lock. Locker rental fees can be added to your monthly e-pay membership fee, or you may pay your locker fee quarterly at the front desk. If fees are not paid, personal belongings and lock will be removed from the locker.

There are also lockers reserved for day use at no charge. Personal locks and belongings not removed by the end of the day will be removed.

Kid Zone

Our kid zone is a supervised play area where your children can play safely for up to 2 hours while you are using our facility. All of our friendly Kid Zone staff members are certified in CPR and first aid to ensure each child's safety.

Kid's Zone Hours

Monday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 3:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Tuesday-Thursday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 3:30 PM - 8:00 PM

Friday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 3:30 PM - 7:30 PM

Saturday: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Kid's Zone Rules

  • Each child may remain in the kid zone for up to 2 hours.
  • Parents MUST remain in our facility at all times while your children are using the Kid Zone.
  • With the exception of baby bottles, no food or drink is permitted in the Kid Zone.
  • Parents must sign their children in and out when entering or leaving the Kid Zone.
  • For the health and safety of everyone, we ask that you do not leave your child in Kid Zone if he/she is experiencing any symptoms of illness (fever, rash, runny nose, diarrhea, etc.).
  • Children that consistently disobey or exhibit hostility towards other children and staff may be temporally suspended. Parents will receive written warnings before such action is taken.

Facility Policies

  1. Members must scan their membership card or fingerprint each time they enter the facility. If you forget your card you may sign in at the front desk by providing your first and last name. If you lose your membership card you may purchase a new one for $3.00. Allowing someone, other than yourself, to use your membership card is grounds to have your membership terminated.
  2. No one under the age of 14 is allowed in the facility unless accompanied by an adult. (17 or older)
  3. SFFC reserves the right to suspend or ban individuals chronically disregarding SFFC policies.
  4. Our weight room is only open to individuals age 14 and older. If you have a child who is between the ages of 8 and 14 who wishes to use the weight room, they may sign up to take our Kids Cardio class. This class is a 2 hour course conducted by one of our personal trainers, and will teach your child the rules of the facility and how to appropriately use the cardio equipment. After completion of this course your child may use the weight room provided they are accompanied by an adult. This course does require and extra fee.
  5. Shirts and shoes are required in the lobby area. Athletic shoes and shirts must be worn during use of the facilities workout areas. Street shoes are not permitted on wood floors. We also require that proper swim suits be worn at all times while using our pools-Street Clothes and Thong bathing suits are not allowed.
  6. Only members or paid guests will be allowed past the lobby area.
  7. Harassment, sexual activity, and any other form of inappropriate behavior is strictly prohibited on facility grounds. Anyone displaying hostile behavior towards staff or other members may have his/her membership suspended or revoked.
  8. Registered sex offenders will be denied memberships to this facility.
  9. Profanity is strictly prohibited. Any member or guest found to be using profanity will be asked to leave the facility. Repeated offenses will result in having their membership or guest privileges revoked.
  10. Use of all tobacco products is prohibited while in the building or on facility grounds. If you must use a tobacco product we ask that you only do so in your vehicle and to not litter on SFFC property.
  11. Theft or behavior that results in destruction of property will result in banned from the facility.
  12. Firearms or objects that may be used as weapons are prohibited.
  13. Use or possession alcohol/illegal drugs on SFFC property is prohibited.
  14. Food and beverages are only permitted in the lobby area. The only exception to this rule is that water is permitted in the work out areas provided it is in a plastic spill proof container.
  15. SFFC is not responsible for lost or stolen items. We strongly recommend that you secure your personal belongings in a locker while using the facility.
  16. Child care is limited to 2 hours. This is a free service to members, if the child is not a member the parent must purchase a guest pass for the child.
  17. All children over the age of 3 must use the gender appropriate locker room or the family locker room.
  18. The use of cell phones with cameras and all other camera or video recording devices are PROHIBITED in all bathrooms, locker rooms and changing areas.

Weight Room

  1. Minimum age requirement is 14 years or older.
  2. Proper workout attire must be work, i.e. sneakers, sweat pants, shorts and tee shirts. (Sandals, flip flops, and open toed shoes are not permitted)
  3. Shoes and shirts must be worn at all times.
  4. Please wipe down machines after each use.
  5. Please adhere to 30 minute time limit on all cardiovascular machines if another member is waiting.
  6. Please do not rest on a machine if others are waiting.
  7. Do not remove t.v. remote control from the weight room.
  8. Store all belongings in a locker. SFFC is not responsible for any items left unlocked.
  9. Food is not permitted. Only water bottles are permitted in a spill proof bottle.
  10. Please strip all weight plates from bars and return them to rack when finished.
  11. Please re-rack all dumbbells and do not drop or slam weights.

Gymnasium

  1. Members and guests must wear sneakers during all gymnasium activities.
  2. Proper gymnasium attire is required to participate.
  3. Food is not permitted. Water bottles are permitted in a plastic spill proof bottle.
  4. No spitting, gum chewing, or eating allowed in gymnasium.
  5. Foul language is not tolerated.
  6. All bags and personal belongings must be stored in a locker.

Swimming Pools

  1. For the health of our members and guests we prohibit the use of our swimming pools if you have any open wounds or sores.
  2. Food is not permitted. Only water bottles are permitted in a plastic bottle.
  3. Please shower before entering the pool area.
  4. Emergency doors that lead from the pool area to the lobby are only to be used in an emergency.
  5. Children under the age of 10 must have a parent or guardian present with them on the pool deck. Children under the age of 5 must have a parent or guardian present with them in the swimming pool.
  6. Children may not dive from blocks without adult supervision.
  7. During a thunderstorm our pools remain open however; you must sign a waiver if you choose to remain in the pool during the storm.
  8. Children under the age of 14 are not permitted in the sauna, steam room, or hot tub.
  9. No rough housing or excessive horseplay on the pool deck or in the pool.
  10. Obey lifeguard instructions. Failure to do so may result in suspension from the facility.
  11. Proper swimsuits and footwear must be worn at all times.
  12. For the safety of all patrons we ask that you dry off before entering the locker room. By entering the locker rooms dripping wet you can create puddles or slick spots that pose a risk for falls or injuries.

Guest Passes

Each family membership will be awarded 5 guest passes annually to share with friends and family members. Adult membership will be awarded 3 guest passes annually.

*Guest passes will only be given to those members that have purchased an annual membership or have signed a contract.

Financial Policies

  • Billing
    1. SFFC does not issue refunds on memberships.
    2. When changes are made to your membership, be sure that you keep the yellow copy of the change form. If we should accidentally bill you after you have canceled your membership you will only be refunded up to one month. You are responsible for checking your bank statements.
    3. All change forms must be turned in by the tenth of each month. Failure to do so may result in changes not being made until the following month.
    4. Your membership fees will be deducted from your account on the 15th of each month. If the 15th falls on a holiday or weekend, your payment will be deducted on the following business day.
    5. All annual e-pay memberships will automatically renew at the end of your contract period. If you wish not to renew, you must submit a cancelation form at the front desk before the 10th of the month.
    6. If you decide to cancel your membership before your contract is fulfilled there will be a cancellation buyout of 40% of the remaining balance.
    7. Monthly memberships must be paid on the appointed date each month. There will be a $5 late fee for failure to pay by the due date. Should your membership lapse for more than 30 days you will be required to pay the joining fee again.
  • On Hold Policy
    1. If a medical situation prevents you from using the facility, we can place your membership on hold for a period of up to 3 months. You will need to fill out the proper request form at the front desk and attach a written statement from your doctor.
    2. If you wish to place your membership on hold while you are out of town for an extended period there will be a $10 charge. You may place your membership on hold for a period of up to 4 consecutive weeks annually. Your contract will be extended to compensate for the time your membership was placed on hold.
  • Account Changes
    1. It is your responsibility to turn in new e-pay information should yours change. If your credit card is due to expire, you are responsible for bringing in a new credit card before the tenth of the month.
    2. Should your address or name change it is your responsibility to provide us with the updated information.
  • NSF Charges
    1. The first time that your bank draft is returned to SFFC from your bank, the NSF fee will be waived. Every time thereafter there will be a $25 NSF fee added to your membership account. Your account will remain frozen until this fee is paid.
  • Contract
    1. You may cancel your contract without penalty by notifying our facility in writing within 3 business days after signing the contract. Cancellation notification must be delivered by certified or registered mail postmarked by midnight of the third business day. To cancel the contract after the third business day, you must provide a statement that you are moving or a medical statement from a doctor. To qualify for cancellation due to relocation, you must be moving at least 35 miles away from SomersetFamilyFitnessCenter and have proof of your new address. Canceling your contract for any other reason other than medical or relocation will result in a cancellation fee of 40% of the remaining balance of your contract.
  • Financial Aid
    1. SFFC is proud to offer a Financial Aid program to families and adults who may not be able to afford regular membership fees. In order to qualify for Financial Aid you must bring in proof of income for each adult that will be on your membership. When you bring in your proof of income you must fill out a Financial Aid form and turn it in to our director for review. This process usually takes 7-10 business days. You may call the facility after 10 business days to see if you were approved and what your membership rate will be. You will be required to reapply annually.
