About Us

General Information

Whether you want to spend the day with your family swimming in one of our two indoor, heated, pools, shooting hoops with your son, or just want to spend a couple hours working out in our state of the art fitness center.

The Somerset Family Fitness Center is available for anybody in the community to use regardless of membership status. To ensure that everyone in our community can use our facility we offer daily guest passes that give you all of the perks of being a member including participation in any of our over 60 fitness classes, year round use of our two indoor pools, relaxing in our sauna, steam room, and whirlpool, use of our weight room, and more all for a nominal fee.

Fitness Classes

  • Cardio Mixer

    A combination of hi/lo aerobics, step, dance, thera-bands, weights, cycling, and stretching. If you're looking to work up a sweat then this is the class for you.

  • Cardio Sculpting

    An intense, total body, workout targeting every muscle group by using light weights and dumbbells. No stepping, no dancing, just muscle pumping.

  • Core + Toning with Weights

    30 minutes of intense, Pilates-style, core work followed by a quick, intense, total body workout. This class targets every muscle group using barbells and dumbbells.

  • Cycling

    Spin your way to improved cardiovascular endurance and upgrade your anaerobic threshold with 60 minutes of cycling on a stationary bike.

  • Fusion

    This class provides a portion of some of the best in fitness – with 30 minutes of core work and 30 minutes devoted to Yoga – to align, strengthen, and lengthen your entire body.

  • Kickboxing

    Kick, jab, punch, shuffle, and uppercut your way to a body that endures. This class will raise your heart rate and improve your balance and coordination. Come ready to work.

  • *Zumba

    A fun class that fuses hypnotic rythym and tantalizing moves to create a dynamic, fun, and easy to do workout. Interval-training sessions sessions with fast and slow rythym are combined to sculpt your body while helping you burn fat. Come join us and have fun while dancing your way to better health.

  • *Zumba Toning

    A muscular endurance plus cardiovascular class, designed to blast calories and challenge your body in new ways.

  • *Zumba Gold

    Takes the Zumba formula and modifies the moves and pacing to suit the needs of the active older participant, as well as those just starting their journey to fitness.

*requires additional fee

Aquatic Classes

  • One-On-One Swimming Lessons

    One-on-one swimming lessons are currently available by appointment. Please call to schedule swimming lessons today!

  • Aqua Fun and Fit

    This is a great class for those looking to incorporate low-impact cardiovascular and toning training into their fitness plan. Morning classes are held in the shallow end of our lap pool and evening classes are held in our heated therapy pool.

  • Aqua Power

    The only NO IMPACT fitness class available. Held in the deep end of our pool this class will have you doing water aerobics while treading water.

  • Arthritis Foundation Aquatic Program

    This class is designed to help you improve the funcation and performance of your daily tasks, mobility, independence, flexibility, balance, and coordination. This class is held in our heated therapy pool, is taught by a certified AFAP instructor, and is great for beginners and those suffering from Arthritis, Fibromyalgia, and MS.

  • Senior Splash

    A water fitness class that can be as hard, or as easy, as you want to make it. Held in our heated therapy pool this class focuses on cardio, flexibility, and balance. A great class for arthritis and fibromyalgia sufferers.
