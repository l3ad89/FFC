Aquatic Classes

Home
  • One-On-One Swimming Lessons

    One-on-one swimming lessons are currently available by appointment. Please call to schedule swimming lessons today!

  • Aqua Fun and Fit

    This is a great class for those looking to incorporate low-impact cardiovascular and toning training into their fitness plan. Morning classes are held in the shallow end of our lap pool and evening classes are held in our heated therapy pool.

  • Aqua Power

    The only NO IMPACT fitness class available. Held in the deep end of our pool this class will have you doing water aerobics while treading water.

  • Arthritis Foundation Aquatic Program

    This class is designed to help you improve the funcation and performance of your daily tasks, mobility, independence, flexibility, balance, and coordination. This class is held in our heated therapy pool, is taught by a certified AFAP instructor, and is great for beginners and those suffering from Arthritis, Fibromyalgia, and MS.

  • Senior Splash

    A water fitness class that can be as hard, or as easy, as you want to make it. Held in our heated therapy pool this class focuses on cardio, flexibility, and balance. A great class for arthritis and fibromyalgia sufferers.

Silver Sneakers

Home

Somerset Family Fitness Center is excited to offer the SilverSneakers Fitness Program, the nation's leading exercise program designed exclusively for older adults. SilverSneakers includes an unlimited, free, membership that includes the use of the fitness equipment, indoor pools, fitness classes, and other amenities, optional low-impact SilverSneakers classes taught by certified instructors, monthly social events and much more!

Have fun and move to the music through a variety of exercises designed to increase muscular strength, range of movement, and activity for daily living skills. Hand held weights, elastic tubing with handles, and a ball are offered for resistance; and a chair is used for seated and/or standing support.

This program is designed to provide a fun and challenging way for you to continue the lifelong journey of holistic health. The program is easily personalized to address multiple fitness levels and differences in health needs. Contact us to find out if your Supplemental Medicare health plan includes SilverSneakers at no additional cost to you.

Facility Policies

Home

Welcome to the SomersetFamilyFitnessCenter, we are so glad you are here! Everyone who enters our facility is considered a valuable member of our SFFC family. This includes the people in our many programs, the volunteers who donate their time and talents, those who support us throughout the community and, of course all of our valued members. Whatever your goals may be by joining our facility, our staff wants to help you achieve them!

Member Services and Amenities

Members of our facility have full access to our weight room, gymnasium, outdoor walking track, basketball court, Kid Zone, swimming pools, hot tub, sauna, steam room, and non-fee based fitness classes. Additionally, you will receive discounted rates on most of our fee based classes and an annual allotment of guest passes to share with family and friends.

Locker Rental

Members may rent a small locker for $5 monthly or a large locker for $10 per month. Locks are provided by the facility or if you choose to do so, you may provide your own lock. Locker rental fees can be added to your monthly e-pay membership fee, or you may pay your locker fee quarterly at the front desk. If fees are not paid, personal belongings and lock will be removed from the locker.

There are also lockers reserved for day use at no charge. Personal locks and belongings not removed by the end of the day will be removed.

Kid Zone

Our kid zone is a supervised play area where your children can play safely for up to 2 hours while you are using our facility. All of our friendly Kid Zone staff members are certified in CPR and first aid to ensure each child's safety.

Kid's Zone Hours

Monday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 3:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Tuesday-Thursday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 3:30 PM - 8:00 PM

Friday: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 3:30 PM - 7:30 PM

Saturday: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Kid's Zone Rules

  • Each child may remain in the kid zone for up to 2 hours.
  • Parents MUST remain in our facility at all times while your children are using the Kid Zone.
  • With the exception of baby bottles, no food or drink is permitted in the Kid Zone.
  • Parents must sign their children in and out when entering or leaving the Kid Zone.
  • For the health and safety of everyone, we ask that you do not leave your child in Kid Zone if he/she is experiencing any symptoms of illness (fever, rash, runny nose, diarrhea, etc.).
  • Children that consistently disobey or exhibit hostility towards other children and staff may be temporally suspended. Parents will receive written warnings before such action is taken.
